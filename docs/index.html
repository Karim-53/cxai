Compare-xAI

A benchmark for feature importance explainable AI

Abstract

Are you wondering what is the difference between public explainable AI (xAI) tools?
Compare-xAI helps you:
    1. shortlist xAI that fits your needs
    2. Evaluate them using an intuitive scoring method
    3. Select one xAI and learn more about its specific requirements
    4. Learn the limits of the interpretability of its output

 Don't forget to cite our paper :)
